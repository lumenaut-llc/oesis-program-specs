# v1.5 product surface

## Purpose

Describe the minimum bridge-stage product surfaces that should exist before the
project claims response measurement rather than only parcel status.

## Primary bridge surfaces

- house-state surface
- intervention log surface
- verification or outcome surface
- evidence view that includes indoor-response and operating-state context

## What the product should be able to show

The bridge-stage product should let an operator inspect:

1. current hazard or parcel condition
2. current house state
3. observed equipment posture where relevant
4. recorded household or system-suggested action
5. measured outcome over a bounded response window

## First serious proof path

Smoke protection should remain the first serious closed-loop proof:

- outdoor evidence
- indoor PM / temperature / RH
- recirculation, fan, or purifier posture where available
- action log
- measured indoor improvement over a bounded window

## Guardrails

- do not present this as a mature adaptation engine
- do not make compatibility inventory the center of gravity
- do not imply that every action is automated

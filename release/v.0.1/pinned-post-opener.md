## Do not add

Do not add these themes to the pinned opener:

- secrets, credentials, or operator-only procedures
- non-cleared third-party data or private parcel-linked data outside the intentional v1 public dataset scope
- unreleased hardware specifics
- claims that the project replaces official alerts or emergency services

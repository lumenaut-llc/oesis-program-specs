# v0.3 Scope Matrix

## Purpose

Map the v0.3 release surface against the v0.2 baseline.

## Sign-off sentence

**v0.3** means: flood-node observations normalized through canonical ingest,
flood conditions reflected in parcel-state where a flood-node is installed —
without claiming response logic, sump monitoring, or intervention surfaces.

## Explicit non-goals for v0.3

- Sump pump monitoring or equipment-state observation
- Flood intervention logic or response verification
- Full three-hazard closed loops (v1.5 capability stage)
- Weather-PM or thermal observation families
- Trust scoring or deployment-quality flags
- Any governance enforcement beyond architectural convention

# v0.2 Scope Matrix

## Purpose

Map the v0.2 release surface against the v0.1 baseline, so reviewers can tell
what is new, what carries forward, and what remains deferred.

## Sign-off sentence

**v0.2** means: bench-air + mast-lite bound to one parcel, both streams
normalized and combined, parcel view explains source mix — without claiming
field-hardened deployment, governance enforcement, or flood/thermal coverage.

## Explicit non-goals for v0.2

- Field-hardened outdoor deployment (bench-only mast-lite is acceptable)
- Governance enforcement beyond architectural convention
- Any observation family beyond air quality (no flood, thermal, equipment-state)
- Trust scoring or deployment-quality flags
- Any shared-map or neighborhood signal surfaces
- Any intervention or verification surfaces

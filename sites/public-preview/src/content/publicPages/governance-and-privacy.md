## Ownership

Homeowners own their raw parcel-linked data. Platform operation does not imply a blanket transfer of ownership.

## Sharing

Sharing beyond the parcel stays opt-in. Later versions extend that rule instead of replacing it.

In practice, that means a larger network can grow without treating private parcel data as public by default.

## Claims

Parcel-state outputs are estimates, not guarantees. The project does not claim emergency authority or official alerts.

In practice, outputs may support household decisions, but they do not replace official alerts, on-scene conditions, or personal judgment.

## Release boundary

The public site does not include held-back technical detail, and open release of software or docs does not turn real homeowner-contributed parcel-linked data into open data.

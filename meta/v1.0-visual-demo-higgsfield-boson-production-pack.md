# OESIS Final Vision Film - Higgsfield Boson Production Pack

## Purpose

Provide a single, generation-ready production file for creating a target-state visual film of the full OESIS vision: outdoor sensing, signal transfer, platform fusion, and parcel-facing display.

This is a vision-first cinematic package, not a maturity-status audit.

## Positioning and On-Screen Disclaimer

Use this exact opening card in the final edit:

`Conceptual target-state demonstration of the intended OESIS end-state architecture and product experience. Module maturity varies by lane.`

## Master Video Settings

- Format: `16:9`
- FPS: `24` or `30` (pick one and keep it consistent)
- Runtime target: `7:00` to `9:00`
- Clip duration target: `6` to `10` seconds
- Output stack:
  - Boson-generated cinematic scenes
  - Optional real UI snippets blended as inserts
- Narrative style: high-clarity documentary-tech vision film
- Rule: add all critical labels in your editor, not in AI-generated text

## Global Style Lock (paste into every Boson prompt)

```text
Cinematic technical vision film, parcel-first environmental intelligence system, high legibility, realistic lighting, deep navy and slate palette with cyan and amber data accents, subtle film grain, refined minimal interface language, calm premium documentary tone, smooth confident camera movement.

Negative constraints: no gibberish text, no random logos, no fantasy holograms, no disaster spectacle, no military aesthetic, no shaky handheld chaos, no heavy glitch effects.
```

## Badge System for This Vision Film

Replace maturity-status badges with vision badges:

- `TARGET CAPABILITY` - intended end-state function
- `ENABLING LAYER` - core technical layer required to support target capability
- `SYSTEM OUTCOME` - user-facing result enabled by the full system

## Editing Blueprint

- Video track V1: Boson vision clips
- Video track V2: optional inserts (real UI, diagrams, schema snippets)
- Video track V3: overlay text and vision badges
- Audio track A1: narration
- Audio track A2: restrained ambient score

### Overlay convention

- Top-left: scene title
- Bottom-left: short explanatory subtitle
- Top-right: one vision badge (`TARGET CAPABILITY`, `ENABLING LAYER`, `SYSTEM OUTCOME`)

### Transition convention

- Use clean dissolves and directional wipes only
- Keep transitions between `10` and `18` frames
- Avoid hard glitch transitions

## Higgsfield Boson Generation Workflow

1. Generate each scene in `3` variants minimum.
2. Pick the best variant and lock style cues before generating the next scene.
3. Reuse consistent prompt structure and motion family to prevent style drift.
4. Export highest-quality intermediates available.
5. Assemble and perform one narrative pass for pacing.

## Camera and Motion Consistency Rules

- Core movement families:
  - `Dolly In`
  - `Slow Orbit`
  - `Lateral Track`
  - `Gentle Crane`
- Keep motion smooth and deliberate.
- Avoid aggressive handheld, crash zooms, and excessive speed ramps.

## Voice and Claims Guardrails For Vision Film

### Preferred phrasing

- `target-state`
- `intended end-state`
- `designed to`
- `will enable`

### Avoid phrasing

- `already complete in production`
- `official emergency authority`
- `guaranteed outcomes`

## Quality Control Checklist

- Opening disclaimer card is present.
- Scene badges use vision taxonomy, not maturity taxonomy.
- Outdoor sensing appears clearly in the first third of the film.
- Signal transfer and ingest normalization are shown as distinct stages.
- Platform display, timeline, and governance controls are clearly represented.
- No contradictory claims about production maturity are spoken.
- All overlays are legible at `1080p`.

# Pilot and Research Data Agreement Template

## Purpose

Provide a baseline checklist for pilot or research programs that touch participant-contributed parcel-linked data.

## Required sections

- program name
- responsible operators
- purpose of the pilot or study
- participating data categories
- sharing mode used
- retention period
- deletion and revocation handling
- publication and redistribution rules
- participant notices and consent method
- security and access controls
- incident reporting contact
- result-sharing plan

## Minimum rules

- the purpose must be specific and documented
- participation must be separate from ordinary product use when the data use goes beyond normal service operation
- real parcel-linked data must not be declared open by default
- any publication plan must be reviewed for singling-out and re-identification risk
- revocation must stop future use where operationally possible and clearly explain any limits

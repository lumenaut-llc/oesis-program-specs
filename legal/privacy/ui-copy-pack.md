# UI Copy Pack

## Purpose

Provide MVP-ready product copy for sharing controls, parcel-state presentation, rights requests, and pilot notices.

## Tone rules

- plain language over legal shorthand
- explicit about uncertainty
- explicit about what is private versus shared
- avoid directive safety language
- avoid vague community-benefit claims without concrete explanation

## Parcel overview copy

### Section title

Current parcel conditions

### Section helper text

These are parcel-level condition estimates based on available local, shared, and public inputs. They are not emergency instructions or guarantees.

### Confidence label

Confidence reflects how complete, recent, and consistent the available evidence is.

### Freshness label

Freshness shows how recently the system received usable evidence for this parcel.

### Provenance affordance

Why am I seeing this?

### Provenance helper text

See whether this estimate came from your local devices, shared neighborhood signals, public context, or a combination of those sources.

## Sharing settings copy

### Section title

Sharing and data use

### Section helper text

Your parcel data stays private by default. You can choose whether to contribute certain signals beyond your own parcel view.

### Private-only mode

Label:
- Private only

Description:
- Your parcel data is used to generate condition estimates for your account and is not shared into neighborhood or research views.

### Network assist mode

Label:
- Help improve inference quality

Description:
- Allow selected parcel signals to be used internally for calibration and model-quality improvements. This does not make your exact parcel readings visible to other households.

### Neighborhood aggregate mode

Label:
- Contribute to neighborhood summaries

Description:
- Allow delayed and coarsened indicators from your parcel to contribute to neighborhood-level condition summaries. Other users should not see your exact parcel readings or exact parcel location from this setting alone.

### Research or pilot mode

Label:
- Join research or pilot sharing

Description:
- Allow data use for the specific pilot or research program described to you. This option should explain who is operating the program, how long the data is kept, and whether results may be published.

### Revocation helper text

You can turn off optional sharing later to stop future contributions. Previously aggregated summaries may remain if they no longer identify your parcel.

## Rights request copy

### Export action

Button:
- Export my parcel data

Confirmation text:
- Create a request for a machine-readable export of your parcel data and related condition history.

### Delete action

Button:
- Delete my parcel data

Confirmation text:
- Create a request to delete account-controlled parcel data held by the service, subject to limited security and legal exceptions.

## Shared map copy

### Section title

Neighborhood conditions

### Section helper text

This view shows delayed, aggregated neighborhood-level conditions. Participation is partial, and visible conditions may include both shared homeowner signals and public context.

### Coverage notice

This map does not show every parcel and should not be read as complete neighborhood visibility.

## Pilot participant notice copy

### Short notice

This pilot provides parcel-level environmental condition estimates based on available evidence. It does not provide emergency instructions, safety certification, or guaranteed outcomes.

### Privacy notice

Your parcel data stays private by default. Optional neighborhood or research sharing is separate and can be changed later.

### Support notice

If you have questions, concerns, or want to change sharing settings, use the pilot contact listed in your enrollment materials.

## Copy to avoid

- safe to evacuate
- safe to re-enter
- your property is safe
- verified safe
- anonymous by design
- full neighborhood visibility
- real-time certainty

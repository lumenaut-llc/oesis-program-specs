# Pilot Participant Notice

## Purpose

Provide a draft plain-language participant notice for a first pilot block.

## Draft notice

Open Environmental Sensing and Inference System is a parcel-first environmental
sensing and parcel-awareness pilot. The system uses available evidence from
local devices, optional shared neighborhood signals, and public context to
generate parcel-level condition estimates.

This pilot does not provide emergency instructions, safety certification, or guaranteed outcomes. Conditions can change quickly, and missing or stale evidence can reduce confidence.

Your parcel data stays private by default. If this pilot offers optional neighborhood or research sharing, those choices should be explained separately. Optional sharing should describe what data leaves the private parcel context, who may see it, what it is used for, and how to turn it off later.

You should be able to review your sharing settings, request export of your parcel data, and ask questions or raise concerns through the pilot support contact.

## Short version

- parcel-level condition estimates, not emergency instructions
- private by default
- optional sharing explained separately
- support, export, and revocation paths should be available
